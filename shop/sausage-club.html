The Ricci Sausage Club.

The Sausage Club is for the people who already know. Every month we ship 3 lbs of a rotating cut — sweet, hot, fennel-heavy, the spicy seasonal — with a member-only recipe card and early access to limited drops. Cancel anytime, no contract.
