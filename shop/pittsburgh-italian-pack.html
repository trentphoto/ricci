How to cook it

Three Ways to Cook It.

Sunday gravy for six

Brown all three meats in batches. Drop into 4 quarts of simmering San Marzano sauce. Simmer 90 min covered, 30 min uncovered. Serve over rigatoni with bread.

Sandwich night

Hot sausage with peppers and onions on Mancini's. Sweet sausage with sauce and Grande. Meatball with sauce and cheese. Three sandwiches, one family.

Freezer strategy

Portion into 1 lb bags before freezing — pull what you need for any given dinner. The bundle covers four to six meals.

On the table

Ideas From the Counter.

A complete Italian Sunday: gravy with all three meats over rigatoni, garlic bread, salad, red wine. Or split it across the week — sausage and peppers Monday, meatball subs Wednesday, sausage pizza Friday.
