About this product

Homemade Italian Meatballs.

Tender, well-seasoned, never dense. Beef, pork, ricotta, parmesan, fresh parsley, garlic, breadcrumbs — the way an Italian household makes them when they expect you for dinner. We roll every meatball by hand in McKees Rocks, in small batches, with the same hand-mixed family spice blend.

Ingredients

Beef, pork, ricotta, parmesan, eggs, breadcrumbs, garlic, parsley, salt, black pepper, family spice blend. All natural — no fillers, no MSG, no additives, no preservatives.

How to cook it

Three Ways to Cook It.

In the oven

Thaw, place on a sheet pan, 400°F for 18–20 min. Transfer into simmering sauce to finish.

Direct into sauce

Drop frozen meatballs into simmering San Marzano sauce. 45 min covered, 15 min uncovered. They release their fat into the gravy.

Pan-fried

Brown all sides over medium heat in a little olive oil, 8–10 min. Finish in sauce or serve straight.

On the table

Ideas From the Counter.

On Mancini's bread with sauce and Grande cheese — that's the meatball sandwich at the counter. Over rigatoni or spaghetti. As an appetizer with toothpicks. In a Sunday gravy with sausage and braciole.
